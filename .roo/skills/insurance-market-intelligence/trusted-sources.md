# Trusted Sources - Insurance Market Intelligence (Brazil)

## Regulators and Official Bodies

- ANS (Agencia Nacional de Saude Suplementar)
  - https://www.gov.br/ans/pt-br
- SUSEP (Superintendencia de Seguros Privados)
  - https://www.gov.br/susep/pt-br
- CNSP (Conselho Nacional de Seguros Privados)
  - https://www.gov.br/fazenda/pt-br/acesso-a-informacao/participacao-social/conselhos-e-orgaos-colegiados/cnsp
- Receita Federal (for pension/tax context when relevant)
  - https://www.gov.br/receitafederal

## Crawl Focus Areas

For each source, prioritize crawling:

1. News / Comunicados / Imprensa pages
2. Product pages with eligibility and coverage details
3. FAQ and regulatory update pages
4. Pricing tables or campaign pages (if public)

## Verification Rule

A commercial claim is accepted only when:

- confirmed by at least one official source, or
- confirmed by two independent reputable sources when official source is absent
